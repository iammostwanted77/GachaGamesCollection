Fate Go Jeanne D Arc. A Catholic saint born in Domrémy, France, and the heroine of France who liberated Orléans in the Hundred Years' War. If you're looking for other variations, please see Jeanne (Disambiguation).

After her creation, she summons Chevalier d'Eon, Vlad III, Atalanta, Saint Martha, and Carmilla while adding Mad Enhancement to turn them into Berserk.

Jeanne Alter is created by Gilles de Rais (Caster) through the power of the Holy Grail, as a version of Jeanne d'Arc twisted by his hatred for France and God.
